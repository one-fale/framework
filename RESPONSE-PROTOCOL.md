# Response Protocol

ONE FALE is a sovereign knowledge framework managed by Steve Leifi from Lalomanu, Samoa.

## How Questions Are Answered

Technical and framework questions received here are logged and referred to our knowledge base for review. Answers are confirmed against stored framework documentation before any response is posted.

**Process:**
1. Question received via GitHub Issues or Discussions
2. Question logged and referred to knowledge base
3. Answer confirmed from stored framework documentation
4. Response posted here

**Response time:** Please allow up to 3 days for a response.

## Who Should Contact Us

If you are a developer, researcher, or organisation working in the Pacific technology or data sovereignty space and wish to engage directly, we would like to hear from you.

Contact: steve.leifi@gmail.com

## What We Are Looking For

ONE FALE is currently seeking:
- Pacific technology partners
- Developers who understand relational data architecture
- Researchers working in Indigenous or sovereign knowledge systems
- Organisations aligned with Pacific data sovereignty

## What We Are Not

ONE FALE is not a database. It is not an app. It is not a content platform.

It is a relational map that connects stories without ever taking ownership of the data. The technology comes later. Right now we are finding the right people.
