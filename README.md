# ONE FALE

**A sovereign knowledge framework that holds all stories under one roof.**

> "The strength of the Fale is not found in the thickness of the timber, but in our relationships."

---

## What is ONE FALE?

ONE FALE is a sovereign knowledge governance framework rooted in Samoan cultural architecture. It was built to solve a problem that existing knowledge systems have not solved: how do you hold many stories, many voices, and many truths together — without forcing them into a single version?

We believe the answer is not a better database. It is found in strengthening the bonds that lead to better relationship.

ONE FALE does not own, store, or process your data. It maps it — indexing relationships while your data stays exactly where it is. You hold the access keys. Always.

ONE FALE is a Protocol for Narrative Sovereignty. It’s not a database that stores stories; it’s a relational map that connects them without ever taking ownership of the data."

## The 10 Principles

1. We learn together.
2. We protect each other.
3. We guide each other.
4. We seek truth in all things, big and small, in pursuit of peace.
5. No doors, many doors.
6. Sharing is learning; we pass on what we learn.
7. We respect boundaries; we withdraw, we don't impose.
8. We honour the ancestors and the next generation.
9. ONE FALE is timeless, where time is a circle.
10. We honour the silence, the space between where all things are held.
